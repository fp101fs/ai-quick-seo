# AI SEO Case Studies: How 3 Brands Improved Rankings 30%+ in 2024

Data-driven SEO wins tell the real story. Here are three case studies showing how combining [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console) data with AI-driven strategies delivered measurable results in 2024.

Each case follows the same process: identify the gap in Search Console, use AI to close it, measure the result.

## Case Study 1: Local Service Business — CTR from 1.2% to 5.8%

**The problem:** A plumbing company wanted more local leads. Their Search Console showed high impressions for "emergency plumber" but only 1.2% CTR — far below the 4–6% expected for position 3.

**The gap:** Their title tag read "Professional Plumbing Services | [Company Name]" — accurate but generic. No urgency, no local signal.

**The AI solution:** Used AI to rewrite titles and meta descriptions with urgency and specificity:

- Title: "Emergency Plumber Available Now — Same Day Service in [City]"
- Meta: "Call [number] for emergency plumbing 24/7. Licensed, insured, 1-hour response time."

**Result:** CTR jumped to 5.8%. Inbound calls increased 35% over 6 weeks.

**Lesson:** For local and service businesses, urgency + specificity in the title tag is the single highest-leverage change you can make. See how to find these opportunities in [SerpDo's Opportunities page](/opportunities).

## Case Study 2: E-commerce Product Pages — 12 Pages to Page 1

**The problem:** An online retailer had 50 product pages stuck on page 2. Search Console showed users searched for specific product features ("waterproof," "20-hour battery") — but the page titles only used generic product names.

**The gap:** Intent mismatch. Searchers wanted features; titles offered brand names.

**The AI solution:** Used AI to rewrite titles and descriptions around feature-based keywords:

- Before: "Bluetooth Speaker Model X200"
- After: "Waterproof Bluetooth Speaker — 20-Hour Battery, 360° Sound"

**Result:** 12 pages moved to page 1 within 8 weeks. Organic revenue increased 22% quarter over quarter.

**Lesson:** E-commerce CTR wins come from matching titles to *how users search*, not how internal teams name products. The [AI Content Gap Analysis](/blog/article-4-ai-content-gap-analysis) process works especially well here.

## Case Study 3: Blog Publisher — 31% Traffic Lift from FAQ Sections

**The problem:** A food blog had 200 posts but only 20 drove meaningful traffic. Search Console revealed many posts ranked for long-tail recipe queries (like "how long to bake sweet potatoes at 400F") but had high bounce rates and low dwell time.

**The gap:** The posts answered the main recipe question but ignored the follow-up questions users search for immediately after.

**The AI solution:** Used AI to identify the 5 most common follow-up questions for each recipe (visible in Search Console as related queries), then added FAQ sections directly in each post:

- "How long do sweet potatoes take to cook?"
- "Can I bake sweet potatoes at 425°F?"
- "How do I know when sweet potatoes are done?"

**Result:** Overall blog traffic increased 31%. Average session duration rose from 1.2 to 2.4 minutes. Several posts started appearing in [Google AI Overviews](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/ai-overviews) due to the structured FAQ content.

**Lesson:** FAQ sections built from real Search Console queries are one of the highest-ROI content additions for blog publishers. [SerpDo's AI Coach](/coach) can suggest these based on your specific keyword data.
